"One Bowl Chocolate Cake"

We all love to treat ourselves once in a while, why not treat yourself with this one bowl Chocolate cake?

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups White Sugar
  • 1 3/4 Cups all-purpose flower
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Step by Step Instructions:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl
  2. Mix in the eggs, vanilla and oil on medium speed. Stir in the boiling water
  3. Pour the batter into prepared pans. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean.
