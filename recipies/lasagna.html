"Worlds Best Lasagna"


Want to make Lasagna? Why not make the worlds best Lasagna! and we will tell you how!

Step by Step Instructions:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the reciple instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
